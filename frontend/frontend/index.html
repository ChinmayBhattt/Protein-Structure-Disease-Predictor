About ProteinAI

ProteinAI is an advanced web-based platform that leverages the power of AlphaFold2 and ColabFold to predict protein structures and analyze disease associations. Our mission is to provide researchers and healthcare professionals with cutting-edge tools to accelerate drug discovery and improve patient outcomes.

With ProteinAI, you can easily predict the 3D structure of proteins, identify potential disease-causing mutations, and obtain detailed analysis reports. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly, secure, and highly efficient.
